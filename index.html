가지도 않을 표를 사는 사람들

Translation: Those Who Buy Tickets They Never Intend to Use

We investigated Korea’s secondary ticket market by reverse-engineering Ticket Bay and building a daily crawler to track listings. Our analysis uncovered how a small group of resellers dominate the market, inflating prices far beyond face value. The interactive site visualizes these patterns, letting you explore resale price gaps by game and even down to seat level.

Python, d3.js

Cold Plunges for Him: When the For You Page Isn't for Me

An analysis of 212 PubMed articles revealed that most cold therapy studies excluded women, yet this bias disappears as findings spread into health media. This project traces how advice based on male physiology gets generalized into universal wellness claims.

Python, d3.js
